The Problem

There is a fundamental disconnect between assigned reading and true understanding.

Students often read in isolation, struggling with complex texts without support. Educators assign chapters with no visibility into true comprehension until it's too late. This silence leaves both students and teachers in the dark.

+
+
How It Works

Simple to use.
Serious in depth.

Chapter is a dedicated reading platform built for Educators. Students engage with assigned readings using purpose-built tools. Those interactions become actionable data for educators.

01

Upload & Define Outcomes

Educators upload texts and define the learning objectives they expect students to meet.

02

Students Actively Annotate

Students read and employ reading tools.

03

Actionable Educator Insights

Analytics help educators make more informed decisions.

The Platform

Built for every stakeholder

Click each feature below to explore a live showcase of our reading environment and analytics dashboards.

Feature 01

Clutter-Free Reader

A focused, clean reading environment designed to minimize cognitive load. Students engage directly with margin tools while distractions are kept out of sight.

Feature 02

Active Annotations

Highlights, margin comments, and student reading milestones are logged in context. These metrics sync silently to populate aggregate datasets.

Feature 03

Educator Analytics

Educators track class progress maps, time-on-page stats, and paragraph highlights. Spot comprehension gaps instantly before class begins.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about integrations, compliance, and how Chapter operates.

What analytics do educators receive?

Educators will have a robust set of analytics that include average reading time, page hotspots (e.g., which pages get the most engagement from students), which concepts and themes students engage the most with and why, and a comprehension accuracy report.

How does Chapter help students?

Chapter provides educators the information necessary to help tailor their efforts toward accommodating students. The goal is to help educators help students.

For what kind of students is Chapter best for?

For Chapter to be successful, it assumes that the student can read. Chapter is not a tool designed to help students to read and develop fluency. Its primary focus to help educators focus their efforts on where students need it most.

When will Chapter be available?

Our team is working daily to ensure that Chapter is available for the year of 2026.
