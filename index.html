How It Works

Simple to use.
Serious in depth.

Chapter is a dedicated reading platform built for academic institutions. Students engage with assigned readings using purpose-built tools. Those interactions become actionable data for educators.

01

Upload & Set Objectives

Professors upload texts and define the learning objectives they expect students to meet, setting a clear benchmark before any reading begins.

02

Students Read & Engage

Students read within Chapter's clean interface, highlighting, annotating, and using reading tools. Every interaction is tracked: time spent, scroll depth, notes taken, re-reads.

03

Professors Receive Insights

Instructors receive aggregate analytics showing how their class engaged with each reading. No individual surveillance. Just the insight needed to understand comprehension gaps and improve instruction.

About Chapter

One thing,
done exceptionally well

Chapter Reading LLC was founded on a straightforward conviction: the most valuable thing a reading platform can offer universities is not content delivery – it's meaningful data about whether students are actually learning.

We do one thing exceptionally well: capturing and presenting aggregate classroom-level comprehension data that helps professors teach better. That singular focus, forged from experience inside university classrooms, drives every product decision we make.

Our Story

Where it all began

Chapter began not as a business idea, but as a personal solution. I originally set out to build a reading platform for my mother – something that would make reading feel less overwhelming and more accessible, while still preserving the value of truly engaging with a text.

That initial intention shaped everything. Instead of creating a tool that shortcuts reading, Chapter was designed to support it – to guide, clarify, and encourage deeper interaction with material.

What started as an effort to help one person read better evolved into a platform built to help anyone approach reading with confidence, structure, and purpose.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about integrations, compliance, and how Chapter operates.

Is Chapter FERPA-compliant?

Yes. Chapter is fully FERPA-compliant. We prioritize student privacy by design, focusing on aggregate comprehension datasets and removing individual student surveillance vectors.

Does Chapter integrate with our existing LMS?

Yes. We integrate seamlessly with major Learning Management Systems, including Canvas, Blackboard, and Moodle, allowing automatic sync of course readings and class rosters.

What file formats does the platform support?

The upload portal supports PDFs, Word documents, and standard web pages. Chapter automatically parses and displays them in our distraction-free reader interface.

How is student reading data tracked?

We track engagement metrics (scroll velocity, focus-page times, and repeat passes) in the background without intrusive trackers or cameras. This is compiled into aggregated classroom-level comprehension heatmaps.
